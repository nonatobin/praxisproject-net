Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 8, 2026

Praxis Project ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website praxisproject.net and provides AI-powered appointment scheduling and communication services for service businesses including salons and spas. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us in connection with our appointment scheduling services:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, and email address
  • Appointment Details: Service type, date, time, and scheduling preferences
  • Communication Records: Call logs and text message history related to appointment management
  • Consent Records: Records of your opt-in consent for voice and SMS communications

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Scheduling, confirming, modifying, and canceling appointments on your behalf
  • Sending appointment reminders and confirmations via SMS or voice call
  • Responding to your inquiries and service requests
  • Maintaining records of your appointment history for the service business you patronize
  • Improving our appointment scheduling services

3. Consent for Communications

We will only contact you via SMS, MMS, or voice call if you have provided explicit, prior consent. By opting in to our messaging services, you agree to receive appointment-related communications at the phone number you provide.

How to Opt In

You may opt in to receive communications by:

  • Calling our service number and verbally confirming your consent
  • Texting a keyword (e.g., "START" or "YES") to our service number
  • Providing consent through a service business that uses our scheduling platform

How to Opt Out

You may opt out at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any SMS message you receive from us
  • Calling us and requesting removal from messaging
  • Emailing us at nonatobin@gmail.com

Once you opt out, we will stop sending you messages within a reasonable timeframe (typically within 24 hours). Opting out of messaging does not cancel any existing appointments.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Businesses: We share your appointment details with the salon or service business where you have booked an appointment, as necessary to provide the scheduling service
  • Service Providers: We use Twilio for voice and SMS communications. Twilio processes your phone number and message content to deliver communications on our behalf, subject to Twilio's Privacy Policy
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. Appointment records are retained for up to 12 months after your last appointment. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at nonatobin@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
